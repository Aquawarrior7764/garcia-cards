How to play

Card Fu is a simple strategy card game based on Club Penguin’s Card-Jitsu. Each round, play a card. Win if your element beats theirs (Water > Fire > Ice > Water), or if same element, higher power wins. Collect 3 winning cards to win the game.

Pro Tips!

  • Track your opponent’s needs and predict their move.
  • Counter their counter-move (or double-bluff them!).
  • Sometimes just go with your gut instinct.
