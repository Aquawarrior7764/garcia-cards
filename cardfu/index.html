How to play

Garcia Adventure is a fast-paced strategy game based on classic Club Penguin card battles. It’s like Rock-Paper-Scissors… but with mind games. Each turn, play a card to try and outsmart your opponent. Win by collecting 3 unique element-color cards, or a set of same elements in unique colors.

Pro Tips

  • Think ahead: Predict what Garcia might throw down next.
  • Mind games: Counter the counter-counter play!
  • Go with your gut: Sometimes simple beats sneaky.
